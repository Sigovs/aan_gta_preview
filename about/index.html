About

Two showrooms, one standard — from the Greater Toronto Area to South Florida.

See the inventory

Built on Referrals,
and Cars Worth Keeping

GTA Motorcars began in the Greater Toronto Area, built on referrals, repeat clients, and cars worth keeping. No volume targets — just the discipline of only buying cars we'd want to own ourselves.

Fort Lauderdale is the second showroom: same people, same standard, warmer weather. Cars move between the two floors, and clients get the reach of both markets with one phone call.
